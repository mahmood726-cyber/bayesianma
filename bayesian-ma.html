Prior for Grand Mean (mu)

Normal prior: mu ~ N(m0, s0²). A vague prior uses large s0 (e.g., 10). A skeptical prior centers at 0 with moderate s0.
Vague: N(0, 10²)

Prior for Heterogeneity (tau)

Half-Cauchy prior: tau ~ Half-Cauchy(scale). Recommended by Polson & Scott (2012). Scale controls how much heterogeneity is plausible a priori.
500 points (fixed)
